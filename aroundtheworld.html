Things to do when travelling

    When travelling, you should try to do the following:
  1. Explore your destination. See what wonders may your destination may be hiding, like remnants of an ancient city, a jaw-dropping mountaintop, or a nice beach to cool off.
  2. Hungry? Try out the food the culture of your destination has to offer. It can shock you on how good these foods can be!
  3. Tradition is fun! Consider exploring the different cultures and seeing what forms of entertainment are provided at your trip location and engage!
    On the other hand, if you want more locations to visit, here are our top 5, in no order:
  • England - Home to the Royal Palace and London.
  • France - Feeling fancy for a baguette? Go here!
  • Brazil - Home to Rio de Janiero and hosts of various Copa Americas and the 2014 FIFA World Cup!
  • South Africa - Hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup, and is an overall great place to visit when going to Africa
  • Italy - Witness the home of pizza and many other wonders, including the ruins of Pompeii, a city surrounded by water (Venice), and more!

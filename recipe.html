Recipe #1: Tallarines Verdes (Green Spaghetti)

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Basil 1 bunch
Spinach 1 bunch (proportional to spaghetti)
Spaghetti 1 pack
Garlic 1 clove
Cheese or Cream Cheese 2 slices or 1 tablespoon
Cooking Oil 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Prepare a pot with boiling water. Place your strands of spaghetti in there, broken in half or not.

Step 2: While your sphaghetti is boiling, get your your basil and spinach and give them a good wash. Dice them neatly.

Step 3: Open a clove of garlic and dice the garlic. In a seperate saucepan, cover with a small amount of oil and drop in your garlic.

Step 4:After some time, drop in your basil and spinach to the saucepan, along with your dollop of cream cheese or slices of cheese, depending on what you choose. Stir until smooth.

Step 5: Transfer the sauce in your saucepan to your blender and blend for some time to further smooth the sauce. In the meantime, drain your pasta.

Step 6: Mix in the sauce with the spaghetti in the same saucepan, then serve once done to your liking.

Recipe #2: Papa a la Huancaina (Green Spaghetti)

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Orange peppers 2, can be spicy
Condensed Milk 2
Crackers / Bread 1 pack or loaf
Potatoes Depends on serving size, recommended nine
Cheese 1 wedge, recommended Queso Fresco
Salt To your liking

Step 1: In a blender, place your solid ingredients. With your pepper, keep the seeds in if you want spiciness. Sprinkle with salt if you desire.

Step 2: Add milk to the mixture, then blend until smooth.

Step 3: In a seperate pot, boil your potatoes, then peel.

Step 4: Serve by scooping your sauce over the potatoes, recommended with other dishes.

Recipe #3: Hamburgers

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Hamburger Buns 4
Tomato 1 (optional)
Ground Beef 1 pack, if sold in patties: 4
Lettuce 4 leaves (optional)
Cheese 2 slices (optional)

Step 1: Turn on your grill, let it preheat for some time.

Step 2: While pre-heating, take your time to prepare your patties and toppings. Tear your lettuce into leaves that will fit in your bun. Finely dice it if you want to. Slice your tomato into four round slices for your four burgers.

Step 3: Unfreeze your ground beef and form them into circles. That will be your patty to go on the grill. Skip this step if you have pre-made patties, just unfreeze them.

Step 4: Your grill should be heated at this point. Place your patties onto the grill and flip them every so often until evenly cooked to your liking.

Step 5 (optional): Place a slice of cheese on top of your patty to let it melt.

Step 6: Serve by transferring your done patty to a burger bun. Add your toppings and condiments, and eat away.
